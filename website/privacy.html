Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Introduction

CiphereCore ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the CiphereCore application and visit our website at cipherecore.com.

Information We Collect

Zero-Knowledge Architecture: CiphereCore is designed with a zero-knowledge architecture. This means:

  • All encryption and decryption operations occur locally on your device
  • Your encryption passwords and keys are never transmitted to our servers
  • We cannot access your encrypted data under any circumstances
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with 12 rounds of salt before storage

Account Information: When you create an account, we store:

  • Username (required)
  • Password hash (bcrypt, we never store plaintext passwords)
  • Email address (optional)
  • Account creation timestamp

Chat Messages: If you use the chat features with MongoDB:

  • Messages are stored on your configured MongoDB instance (self-hosted or specified)
  • We do not operate a central message server
  • If you enable message encryption, only encrypted ciphertext is stored

Website: Our website does not use cookies, trackers, or analytics services. We do not collect any browsing data.

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity when you log in
  • Manage chat sessions and friend connections
  • Provide application functionality

We never sell, license, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: User accounts, sessions, and messages are stored in a local SQLite database (ciphercore.db) on your machine
  • MongoDB (Optional): If configured, chat data is stored on your specified MongoDB instance. You control the server and the data.
  • Log Files: System logs are stored locally in the logs/ directory and contain operational data only (no encryption keys or passwords)

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All passwords are hashed with bcrypt (12 rounds)
  • Session tokens are generated using cryptographically secure random number generators
  • Rate limiting protects against brute-force attacks (5 attempts, 5-minute lockout)
  • Input validation and sanitization prevents injection attacks
  • HMAC verification ensures message integrity

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — all data is stored locally on your device
  • Delete your data — simply delete the database file and logs directory
  • Export your data — database files can be accessed directly
  • Modify your account information through the application

Third-Party Services

CiphereCore uses the following third-party libraries, none of which collect user data:

  • Python cryptography library (encryption operations)
  • bcrypt (password hashing)
  • CustomTkinter (GUI framework)
  • PyMongo (MongoDB driver, optional)

Children's Privacy

CiphereCore is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at cipherecore@gmail.com.
