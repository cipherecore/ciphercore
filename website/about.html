We believe that everyone deserves access to powerful encryption and secure communication tools, regardless of their technical background or financial resources. CiphereCore was born from this conviction.

Our Values

What Drives Us

Privacy First

Your data is yours. We employ zero-knowledge architecture — your keys and passwords never leave your device. We can't access your encrypted data, and we wouldn't want to.

Transparency

We use well-established, peer-reviewed encryption algorithms. No proprietary "black box" cryptography. Every algorithm and its implementation can be inspected and verified.

Accessibility

Professional-grade security shouldn't require a PhD in cryptography. CiphereCore is designed to be intuitive and accessible to everyone, from beginners to security experts.

Free Forever

CiphereCore is and will always be free. No premium tiers, no feature gates, no subscriptions. Security is a fundamental right, not a premium product.
